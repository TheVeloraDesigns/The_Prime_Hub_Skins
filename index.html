Why Choose Us

The Prime
Difference

3M Grade Material

Military-grade 3M vinyl with self-healing properties. Micro-scratches vanish under light heat, keeping your skin flawless for years.

Heat-Dispersing Tech

Nano-ceramic heat channels embedded in our skins actively dissipate thermal build-up, maintaining peak device performance under load.

Zero-Residue Removal

Our pressure-sensitive adhesive peels clean every time — no sticky residue, no surface damage. Change your look whenever you desire.

Precision Laser Cut

Every skin is laser-cut to within 0.1mm of your device's exact dimensions. Ports, cameras, speakers — all exposed perfectly.

Bubble-Free Guarantee

Our air-release channel technology ensures a seamless, bubble-free installation every single time. Frustration-free, guaranteed.

Premium Prints

UV-stable inks printed at 1440 DPI resist fading, yellowing and peeling — colours remain vivid even after years of daily handling.

Our Story

The Prime
Journey

From a passion project in a garage to India's go-to destination for premium device skins.

2017
The Beginning
Started as a small passion project crafting custom mobile skins for friends and family in Lucknow, with a single vinyl cutter and a dream.
2018
First 1,000 Orders
Crossed the milestone of 1,000 orders shipped pan-India. Upgraded to a professional 3M material supply chain and launched our online storefront.
2019
Laptop & Console Skins
Expanded the catalogue to include laptop wraps and PlayStation skins — becoming one of the first Indian brands to offer precision-cut console skins.
2021
10,000+ Customers
The post-lockdown surge in device purchases drove massive growth. Crossed 10,000 happy customers and introduced our Heat-Dispersing Tech series.
2023
Accessories & Wearables
Launched the Accessories line — skins for earbuds, smartwatches and charging bricks. Partnership with 3M India for certified Grade-A vinyl supply.
2025
Prime Skin Hub Relaunch
A full brand evolution — new identity, new website, 500+ device models supported and over 1 lakh skins delivered. The journey is just beginning.
